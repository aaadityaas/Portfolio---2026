Cricket season

IPL Run Chase

ObjectiveGamify payments around the Indian Premier League

How it workedEvery payment earned runs. Users accumulated runs, ranked on a live leaderboard, and top performers won exclusive IPL-themed rewards.

Design challengeDesign an entire campaign ecosystem — entry points, confirmation states, leaderboard UI, rewards catalogue, and parallel micro-campaigns — without cluttering the core payment flow.

What I focused onBringing cricket energy into a financial app without feeling gimmicky. Real-time leaderboard competitiveness, desirable rewards, and seamless integration within existing app architecture.

OutcomeFull-scale engagement campaign running parallel to IPL season

Year-end

Year in Review (Wrapped)

ObjectiveCreate a shareable, personalised year-end experience

How it workedQuiz-style flipbook with swipeable cards revealing personalised insights: top spend categories, biggest transactions, most-used features.

Design challengeShip in a short runway. The entire interaction model — flipbook format, reveal animations, copy tone — was designed and built by the design team with no external reference.

What I focused onMaking each card a small surprise. Pacing, visual hierarchy, and the arc from small facts to satisfying summary were deliberate design decisions.

OutcomeShipped to entire user base

February

Valentine's Day Cross-Sell

ObjectiveSurface relevant products at an emotionally resonant moment

How it workedDifferent user segments shown different cross-sell products wrapped in an interactive Valentine's Day experience.

Design challengeMake cross-sell feel contextual and intentional, not intrusive. Personalisation by segment was critical.

What I focused onJustifying the interactive layer. The design had to prove it earned user attention, not just add noise.

OutcomeStrong CTR and meaningful conversions across segments
